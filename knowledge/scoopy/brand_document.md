- Homeowners with gardens in Dundee, Perth, Forfar, Fife

- Garden has become unusable (kids can't play safely)

6. **Scottish Weather:** Makes DIY cleanup even more miserable
7. **Family Tension:** Arguments about whose job it is
8. **Deep Shame:** "How did I let it get this bad?"

**WHAT THEY NEED:**
- Transformation not maintenance
- Professional rescue that handles "too much" buildup
- Zero judgment from service provider
- Before/after proof it's solvable
- Relief that someone will fix this
- Ongoing service so it never gets bad again
- Affordable rescue package (£59 + first week free)

**DECISION DRIVERS:**
- "Can they actually handle THIS much?"
- "Will they judge me?"
- "How fast can they fix this?"
- "Can I afford the rescue?"
- "Will it stay clean after?"

**MESSAGING TONE:**
- Empathetic but competent

### HOW CAMPAIGNS WORK TOGETHER (Reciprocal Priming)

**When customers see BOTH:**

**Rescue primes Maintenance:**
- See "problem state" → feel smart for not being that bad
- Creates urgency "don't let it get that bad"
- Builds brand awareness (entertaining, memorable)

**Maintenance primes Rescue:**
- See "solution state" → highlights the gap between where they are and could be
- Shows ongoing service exists (not just one-time)
- Makes rescue feel like bridge to normal life

**Facebook optimizes delivery:** People engaging with rescue content see more rescue. People engaging with maintenance see more maintenance. Both running = self-optimizing funnel.
